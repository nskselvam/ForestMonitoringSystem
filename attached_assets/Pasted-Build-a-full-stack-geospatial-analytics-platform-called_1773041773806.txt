"SENTINEL-2 AUTOMATED LAND CHANGE MAPPING SYSTEM"

DATA SOURCE

Sentinel-2 Satellite Imagery

Resolution: 10 meters per pixel

Bands used:

B04 = Red
B08 = Near Infrared
B11 = SWIR

These bands are used to compute vegetation and urban indices.

User selects:

Start Year
End Year

2022 → 2026

Then the system must:

1. Fetch Sentinel-2 imagery for Tamil Nadu
2. Extract spectral bands
3. Compute NDVI vegetation index
4. Compute NDBI urban index
5. Classify land cover
6. Detect land use changes
7. Detect hotspots
8. Calculate risk metrics
9. Generate dashboard visualizations

---

SATELLITE INDICES

NDVI (Vegetation Index)

Formula:

NDVI = (B08 − B04) / (B08 + B04)

Purpose

Measure vegetation health
Detect forest loss
Detect agricultural land loss

---

NDBI (Built-up Index)

Formula

NDBI = (B11 − B08) / (B11 + B08)

Purpose

Detect buildings
Detect urban expansion
Detect industrial growth
